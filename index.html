Our Practice

At BrightWell Mental Health, therapy is tailored to meet your unique needs and goals. I integrate evidence-based approaches with compassionate care, creating a safe and supportive environment where healing and growth can occur.

Therapeutic Modalities:

  • Adults & Couples Therapy:
    Supporting individuals and couples in strengthening communication, navigating conflict, and deepening emotional connection.
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT):
    Identifying and challenging negative thought patterns to promote healthier ways of thinking and behaving.
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT):
    Using mindfulness and emotional regulation strategies to improve emotional balance and interpersonal effectiveness.

In every session, I provide empathy, authenticity, and unconditional positive regard. This foundation helps cultivate a strong therapeutic alliance and empowers you to build meaningful coping strategies for long-term well-being.
