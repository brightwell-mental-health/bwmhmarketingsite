Our Practice

At BWMH, therapy is tailored to meet your unique needs and goals. My approach integrates evidence-based methods with compassionate care, creating a safe and supportive environment where healing and growth can occur.

Therapeutic Modalities:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT):
    Helps identify and challenge negative thought patterns to promote healthier thinking and behavior.
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT):
    Combines mindfulness and emotional regulation strategies to support emotional balance and interpersonal effectiveness.

In addition to structured modalities, sessions are grounded in empathy, authenticity, and unconditional positive regard. This foundation helps cultivate a strong therapeutic alliance and empowers individuals to develop meaningful coping strategies for long-term well-being.
